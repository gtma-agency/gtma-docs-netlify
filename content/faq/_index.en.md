{{< faq "How do I submit a task to Web Division?" >}}
Use the following form to submit projects directly into our Asana project management system. A project coordninator will be notified and it will be scheduled. We'll reach out to you when that is done.

[Submit a new Task](https://form.asana.com/?k=JUdzYOW5Hn87CEHzHkC9Ow&d=551955083548302)
{{</ faq >}}

{{< faq "Do we build anything other than WordPress sites?" >}}
No, we're strictly a WordPress shop. Although this site is not on WordPress. But don't take that as hypocritical, we just get bored sometimes and need to explore other options.
{{</ faq >}}
